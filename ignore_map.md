## RULES OF ENGAGEMENT
1. **Recon First**: Read GFG/NeetCode explanation of the *pattern* before coding.  
2. **Pseudocode First**: Write logic in plain English. No Python syntax.  
3. **15-Minute Rule**: Stuck? Look up the *pattern name*, not the full solution.  
4. **Daily Quota = 6**: Non-negotiable. Complete all 6 before sleeping.  
5. **No Skipping**: Confirm understanding before moving to next problem.

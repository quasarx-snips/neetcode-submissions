## 📅 DAY 3: Optimization & The Box (Chain Rule, Max/Min, Lagrange)
### 📐 Math (IAMAT101)
- [ ] **Topic:** Chain Rule, Maxima/Minima, and Lagrange Multipliers
- [ ] **Read:** Strang's Calculus, **Section 13.5, 13.6, & 13.7** (Pages **573–598**)
- [ ] **Focus:** The Second Derivative Test for saddle points vs. local min/max (p. 586). Setting up the Lagrange multiplier equations $\nabla f = \lambda \nabla g$ (p. 591).

### ⚛️ Physics (IAPHY101)
- [ ] **Topic:** Particle in a 1D Box (Infinite Potential Well)
- [ ] **Read:** Applied Physics Notes, **Pages 12–15**
- [ ] **Focus:** Applying boundary conditions ($\psi=0$ at $x=0$ and $x=L$), deriving the quantized energy eigenvalues $E_n = n^2 h^2 / 8mL^2$ (p.13), and the normalized wave function $\psi = \sqrt{2/L} \sin(n\pi x/L)$.
